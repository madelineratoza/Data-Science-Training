# Data Science Training

This repository contains training materials, tutorials, and examples focused on applied data science for health sciences, education, and academic contexts.

## Training Materials

- [Data Science Training Site](https://madelineratoza.github.io/Data-Science-Training/)

The site includes guided examples, reproducible workflows, and instructional content designed for beginners and practitioners transitioning into data science work.

## About

These materials are intended to support:
- Faculty and staff learning foundational data science skills  
- Reproducible analysis and reporting  
- Applied use cases in health professions education and research
